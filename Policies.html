Shipping Policy

Free Standard Shipping on all orders over $50. Most orders ship within 1-2 business days.

Shipping Methods & Timeframes

  • Standard Shipping (3-5 business days): $4.99 or FREE on orders over $50
  • Express Shipping (1-2 business days): $9.99
  • Overnight Shipping: $19.99 (Order by 2 PM EST for next-day delivery)

International Shipping

We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping costs and delivery times vary by destination.

Return Policy

30-Day Return Policy: We accept returns within 30 days of delivery.

Return Eligibility

  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • All tags and labels must be attached
  • Final sale items are not eligible for return

Refund Policy

Refund Processing: Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Refund Methods

  • Refunds are issued to the original payment method
  • Credit card refunds may take 3-5 business days
